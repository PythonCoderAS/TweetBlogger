Why make this website?

Have you ever browsed a long thread on Twitter and got frustrated trying to browse to the top? Traditionally, one could call {{ twitter_username("threadreaderapp")|safe }} and ask to unroll, but it does not work well when multiple users have created one long chain. It also does not work when instead of being a thread, it's a chain of retweets. This service tries to take these Tweets and make a blog-like page displaying the tweets in chronological order of oldest to newest Tweet.
